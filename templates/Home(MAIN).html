How It Works



1

Registration and Listing



Users (donors, recipients, or organizations) sign up on the app. Donors list surplus food items by specifying details like quantity, type, expiration date, and location

2

Matching and Requests



The app matches listed donations with nearby recipients (e.g., food banks, charities, or individuals). Recipients can view available food donations and place requests

3

Approval and Coordination



Donors review and approve requests. The app facilitates communication for pickup or delivery, offering tracking and time scheduling

4

Delivery and Feedback



The food is picked up by the recipient or delivered by a volunteer/partner service. Both parties can provide feedback to ensure transparency and improve the service.
